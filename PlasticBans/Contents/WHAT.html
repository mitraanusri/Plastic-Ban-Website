WHAT PLASTIC BAGS ARE MADE UP OF?

Plastic bags are made from polyethylene.This begins as ethylene,then treated to become the polymer, forming long chains of carbon and hydrogen atoms.

General Plastics

  • Plastics are created from polymers.
  • Ethylene is chemically changed into polyethylene, the building block of all plastic bags.
  • Polyethylene is made up of many winding chains of carbon atoms, interlaced with and bonded to hydrogen atoms.

Sources

  • Both petroleum and natural gas are common sources and vital ingredients in almost all plastic bags available today.
  • Properly refined, they yield ethylene, which is in turn made into polyethylene.

HDPE Plastic

  • HDPE stands for high density polyethylene, and is the most common type of polyethylene used to create shopping bags.
  • Our grocery bag is light yet can hold many times its own weight without tearing.

LDPE Plastic

  • LDPE plastic is made from low density, branching chains of polymer materials.
  • This is used to make the tear-away bags dry cleaners use for wrapping cleaned clothes.

LLDPE Plastic

  • Known as linear low density polyethylene.
  • The shiny bags are used in clothing stores.
