Plastic bags can not be recycled. The recyclable symbol of three arrows in a circle is on many plastic shopping bags in the US and many other countries. It is simply used as marketing trick. Many plastic bags that are collected by recycling companies cannot really be recycled as most of these bags actually end up in landfills and sit there for hundreds of years.
