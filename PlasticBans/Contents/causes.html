CAUSES

CAUSES

Plain Old Trash

  • Every time one of these items gets thrown away or washed down a sink, the toxic pollutants have more of a chance to enter the environment and do harm.

It is Overused

  • When plastic is used and disposed, it does not decompose easily and pollutes the land or air nearby when burned in the open air.

Fishing Nets

  • The nets are usually made of plastic.
  • They spend long times submerged in water, leaking toxins at will, but they also often get broken up or lost, left to remain wherever they fall.

Disposing of Plastic and Garbage

  • Burning plastic is incredibly toxic, and can lead to harmful atmospheric conditions and deadly illness.
  • The process of recycling plastic can also lead to plastic irritants being released in a number of ways.
