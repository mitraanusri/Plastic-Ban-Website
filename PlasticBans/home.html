ABOUT:

  • Plastic bags pollute our land and water. Being lightweight they blow long distances littering our landscape, floating along our waterways and eventually reaching the oceans.

  • Different countries have different usage levels, but the entire world has to commit to reducing this usage. A plastic bag is made from depletable resources, yet almost never breaks down.

  • Every countries in the world should play their part in an effort to completely ban plastic bags because it is damaging our environment before it is too late.
